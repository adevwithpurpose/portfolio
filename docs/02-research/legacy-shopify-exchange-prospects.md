## TIER-BASED PROSPECT STRATEGY

### Tier 1: High-Ticket Prospects ($100K+ valuations)
**Profile**: Established brands with revenue, manual processes, multi-channel
**Approach**: Direct outreach via broker inquiry (contact seller through listing)
**Where**: Empire Flippers, Acquire.com (curated listings)

### Tier 2: Mid-Market Prospects ($20K-$100K)
**Profile**: Growing stores with scaling challenges, theme/performance issues
**Approach**: Monitor Flippa new listings, reach out within 48hrs of posting
**Where**: Flippa (filter by $20K-$100K, positive cash flow)

### Tier 3: Entry Prospects ($5K-$20K)
**Profile**: New owners, dropshippers, "starter" stores
**Approach**: Volume play - many small improvements, upsell to bigger projects
**Where**: Ecomswap, Investors Club

## OUTREACH APPROACH BY TIER

### Tier 1 (Empire Flippers / Acquire.com)
1. Browse listings matching pain point criteria
2. Use the platform's contact form to inquire about the seller
3. Ask broker to introduce you as a "potential buyer interested in operations"
4. Frame your value as: "I can help optimize before you sell for better valuation"

### Tier 2 (Flippa)
1. Set up Flippa alerts for new Shopify listings
2. Contact sellers within first 48 hours (less competition)
3. Lead with: "I noticed your store - I'm a Shopify developer specializing in [their pain point]"
4. Offer free audit as lead magnet

### Tier 3 (Volume Approach)
1. Look for stores with obvious issues (slow descriptions, poor listings)
2. Cold outreach with specific improvement suggestions
3. Offer fixed-price optimization packages

## CONTACT STRATEGIES

**Finding Owner Emails** (when not visible on listings):
1. **BuiltWith.com** - Look up store URL to see tech stack → identifies their current setup
2. **Hunter.io** - Find emails associated with the store domain
3. **RocketReach** - For business contact info
4. **LinkedIn** - Search for company + "founder/owner"

**Subject Line Ideas**:
- "Quick audit of [store name] - [specific pain point I noticed]"
- "Shopify optimization opportunity - [specific improvement]"
- "Before you sell on [platform] - let me help increase valuation"
