## NEW: AI Opportunity Angles (from Video)

### 1. Ambient Businesses
**What:** Businesses that run with zero or very low daily human input. Agents monitor markets, identify opportunities, execute tasks, handle customer service.

**Client angle:** "Build a business that runs while you sleep"

**Target verticals:**
- E-commerce store owners who want automated inventory + customer service
- Local service businesses (dentists, lawyers, real estate) needing 24/7 lead capture
- SaaS founders wanting internal ops automation

**Pain points we solve:**
- "I check my business once a week and it just works"
- "My team wastes hours on tasks that should be automated"
- "I need AI agents that handle the repetitive so I focus on growth"

### 2. Vertical AI (Headcount Replacement)
**What:** Vertical AI replaces headcount (10x bigger TAM than vertical SAS). YC predicts 300+ unicorns in vertical AI this decade.

**Client angle:** "Replace $80K/year employees with $2K/month agents"

**Target niches (sub-niched):**
- Insurance agencies (policy renewals, claims processing)
- Real estate brokerages (lead qualifying, scheduling, follow-ups)
- Legal firms (client intake, document review, scheduling)
- Logistics companies (tracking updates, customer comms)
- Elder care agencies (client intake, scheduling, reminders)

**Key insight:** These industries run on phone calls + faxes. They're ripe for AI agent disruption.

### 3. Outcome-Based Pricing
**What:** Shift from seat-based → usage-based → outcome-based pricing. Pay per result delivered.

**Client angle:** "You only pay when it works"

**Pricing models:**
- **Per task resolved:** $X per customer service ticket closed
- **Per lead qualified:** $X per lead that books a call
- **Per report delivered:** $X per weekly analytics report
- **Monthly retainer (outcome-guaranteed):** Fixed monthly fee with defined KPIs

**Gartner prediction:** 40% of enterprise SAS shifts to outcome-based by 2030

### 4. Agent Economy Services
**What:** 2025-2030 is the agent economy. CEO agents, sales agents, dev agents, marketing agents hiring each other.

**Client angle:** "Your AI employee that trains, learns, and scales"

**Service offerings:**
- Agent setup + training (one-time)
- Agent management retainer (monthly)
- Agent ecosystem design (for larger clients)
