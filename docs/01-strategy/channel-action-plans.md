# Client Hunting — Channel Action Plans

Maturity: beta
Last updated: 2026-04-27

## Current direction

Use broad, premium positioning for the main portfolio and general outreach:

- custom AI agents
- automation systems
- web systems / platforms
- internal tools
- workflow cleanup and operational visibility

Avoid leading with a specific platform/tool unless the channel or prospect explicitly calls for that niche.

---

## Priority: P1 (Do First)

### 1. Reddit / founder communities — Post services ad

**Why:** High-intent clients browsing for builders. No algorithm dependence.

**Action:** Post a clear “I build systems” ad. Refresh monthly where allowed.

**Draft:**

```text
[For Hire] Automation + Web Systems for Founder-Led Businesses

I help small teams replace messy manual workflows with cleaner digital systems:

• fast portfolio/landing pages that explain the offer clearly
• automation systems for lead intake, follow-up, reporting, and handoffs
• AI assistants/internal tools for repetitive ops work
• dashboards and visibility layers so founders know what is happening

Good fit if your work is stuck across spreadsheets, forms, DMs, and scattered tools.

Project-based. $1K+ preferred. DM with the workflow you want cleaned up.
```

**When:** Post after checking each community’s rules. Refresh only within allowed cadence.

---

### 2. Upwork — Apply to 3 qualified jobs/week

**Why:** High-intent paid demand for automation, AI agents, internal tools, and web systems.

**Filters:**

- $1K+ preferred
- clear business problem
- founder/operator can explain the workflow
- avoid vague “make me an AI SaaS” posts with no scope

**Search terms:**

- workflow automation
- business automation
- AI agent
- internal tool
- dashboard
- CRM automation
- web app
- landing page
- operations automation

**Proposal formula:**

```text
I noticed [specific workflow/problem from their post].

The fastest useful version would be [small concrete system], then we can extend it once it is working.

I can help map the workflow, build the first version, and leave you with a clean handoff/doc so it is maintainable.

Want me to outline a simple first version for your case?
```

---

### 3. LinkedIn — Profile + proof-led posting

**Why:** Decision-makers, founders, ops managers, and service business owners are reachable here.

**Profile direction:**

```text
Automation & Web Systems Builder | AI Assistants, Workflow Cleanup, Internal Tools
```

**About direction:**

```text
I help founder-led teams turn messy workflows into clean digital systems.

What I build:
→ websites and landing pages that explain the offer clearly
→ automation systems for intake, follow-up, reporting, and handoffs
→ AI assistants and internal tools for repetitive operational work
→ dashboards that make the business easier to see and run

Background in mechatronics and field/problem-solving, so I think in systems rather than tool lists.
```

**This week:** Post 1 proof asset or teardown showing a workflow before/after.

---

## Priority: P2 (Do This Week)

### 4. X/Twitter — Build presence carefully

**Why:** Good for proof, short insights, and founder discovery.

**Action:** Post 3-5x/week. Mix:

- 2x workflow teardown / systems thinking posts
- 1x build/proof post
- 1x practical automation insight
- 1x learning-in-public note if it sounds natural

**Hashtags:** Use lightly. Do not make posts look like AI slop.

**Monitor:**

- “manual workflow”
- “need automation”
- “internal tool”
- “dashboard”
- “AI agent for my business”
- “website redesign”

---

### 5. Communities and niche forums

**Why:** Some prospects know their tools; others only know their pain.

**Action:**

1. Answer questions where Safeer can show useful judgment.
2. Lead with the business workflow, not the tool.
3. Save tool-specific pitches for tool-specific communities.

**Look for:** “need help automating”, “manual reporting”, “follow-up is messy”, “dashboard needed”, “too many tools”, “internal process is broken”.

---

### 6. Founder/service-business direct outreach

**Target:** Founder-led businesses, creators, agencies, ops-heavy teams, and service businesses with visible workflow friction.

**DM template:**

```text
Hi [name] — noticed [specific workflow/sign of friction].

I build small automation/web systems that clean up this kind of operational drag: intake, follow-up, reporting, handoffs, and internal visibility.

If useful, I can send a quick 3-point teardown of where the workflow could be simplified.
```

**Volume:** 5-10 high-quality DMs/week. Quality over spray-and-pray.

---

## Priority: P3 (Ongoing)

### 7. Monitor job boards

Check 3x/week:

- Upwork automation / AI agent / internal tool jobs
- Wellfound / startup ops roles that hint at freelance systems work
- Reddit/founder communities where hiring posts are allowed
- Tool-specific job boards only when they match the task

Apply only when the project maps to Safeer’s broad systems positioning or a strategically useful niche opportunity.
