## Subject Line Variants

For cold outreach:
- "Quick audit of [store name] — [specific pain point]"
- "Shopify optimization opportunity — [specific improvement]"
- "Before you sell on [Flippa/Empire] — one thing to fix first"
- "Noticed your store — [specific observation]"

For warm introductions (via broker):
- "Potential buyer intro — Shopify ops specialist"
- "Question about your [Store Name] listing"

---

## Key Principles

1. **Specificity beats generic** — Reference their actual store, their actual pain point
2. **Lead with observation, not pitch** — "I noticed X" > "I help X"
3. **Low friction commitment** — Free audit, 10-min call, video walkthrough
4. **Frame around their goal** — They want to sell/scale; position yourself as the person who gets them there faster
5. **Timing matters** — Contact new Flippa listings within 48hrs (less competition)
