# Experiment: Intent-Signal Acquisition OS Manual Validation

Date: 2026-04-28

Objective: Validate whether Safeer can generate qualified client opportunities by monitoring public buying-intent signals instead of cold email.

Hypothesis: If Safeer checks high-intent sources daily and scores opportunities with a consistent rubric, he can find better-fit prospects than generic cold outreach and build a system that later becomes sellable.

Audience/source:

- Upwork jobs for automation, AI agents, internal tools, dashboards, workflow automation, web apps
- X native searches for “need automation”, “looking for developer”, “internal tool”, “AI agent”, “dashboard”
- Reddit manual checks in approved subreddits after reading rules
- LinkedIn manual feed/job alerts/comment monitoring
- Hacker News / Indie Hackers / founder communities

Allowed actions:

- Manual browsing/searching
- Saving public links
- Copying small excerpts needed for scoring
- Scoring opportunities
- Drafting replies/proposals
- Sending only after Safeer explicitly approves
- Public helpful comments where platform/community rules allow

Forbidden actions:

- Autonomous spam
- Fake personalization
- Auto-DMs
- Bulk messaging
- Scraping LinkedIn
- Bypassing login walls/CAPTCHAs
- Using unofficial X/Reddit scrapers
- Posting in subreddits without reading rules
- Unverifiable claims about results/ROI

Batch size:

- Target 50 captured signals in 7 days
- Minimum 10 P1/P2 opportunities
- Maximum 3-5 approved outreach actions in first week unless Safeer asks to scale

Success metric:

- Primary: at least 3 prospects worth responding to with specific, non-generic drafts
- Secondary: identify top 2 channels by signal quality
- Tertiary: at least 1 conversation started or clear proof that a source produces relevant demand
