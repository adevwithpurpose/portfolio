# AI Agent Client Onboarding Playbook

**For:** Safeer — Freelance AI Agent + n8n + Shopify Developer  
**Target Clients:** E-commerce brands (Shopify) wanting automation + ongoing AI agent management  
**Positioning:** Premium ($150–300/hr), white-label, Service-as-a-Software model

---

## Phase 1: Discovery Call Template

### Pre-Call Research (Before Call)
- [ ] Review client's Shopify store (theme, apps, traffic, top products)
- [ ] Check their social media / LinkedIn for company context
- [ ] Note any public reviews or press mentions
- [ ] Prepare 2–3 specific observations about their current setup

### Discovery Call Agenda (30 min)

**Intro (2 min)**
- Thank them for the call
- Confirm their main pain point from initial outreach
- Set agenda: "Want to understand where you're feeling friction, so I can tell you honestly if/how I can help"

**Questions:**

1. **Operations Assessment**
   - "Walk me through your current fulfillment flow — from order to delivery."
   - "What's the most time-consuming part of running your store?"
   - "Where do things fall through the cracks?"

2. **Volume & Scale**
   - "How many orders are you doing per month?"
   - "How many people are on your team?"
   - "What's your growth target for the next 6 months?"

3. **Current Stack**
   - "What automation do you have now? (n8n, Zapier, Shopify Flow, etc.)"
   - "What apps are you currently paying for?"
   - "Any manual spreadsheets or processes you hate?"

4. **Pain Points (Most Important)**
   - "What's the #1 thing that makes you want to pull your hair out?"
   - "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one process, what would it be?"
   - "What's your tolerance for risk when trying new tools?"

5. **Budget & Timeline**
   - "What's your budget range for solving this?"
   - "When do you need this live by?"
   - "Who else is involved in the decision?"

**Close (3 min)**
- Summarize what you heard
- Give honest assessment (can you help / scope / timeline)
- If yes: "Let me put together a proposal — I'll have it to you by [date]"
- If no: "Based on what you described, I don't think I'm the right fit because [reason]. But here's what I'd suggest..."

## Appendix: Quick Reference

### Discovery Call Red Flags ⚠️
- Client can't articulate their pain point clearly
- No budget or timeline (window shopping)
- Decision by committee (too many stakeholders)
- Already talked to 5 other agencies
- "We just need something simple" (nothing is simple)

### Good Fit Indicators ✅
- Clear pain point they can describe
- Decision maker on the call
- Realistic budget ($1500+/mo for serious projects)
- Growth-oriented (they want to scale)
- Technical awareness (knows what n8n/API means)

### Common Objections + Responses

**"Your rate is higher than others"**
→ "I'm not selling you software — I'm selling you outcomes. My clients see ROI within 60 days or I keep working until they do."

**"We already have a developer"**
→ "I'm not replacing your developer — I'm taking the repetitive work off their plate so they can focus on features that grow revenue."

**"We tried automation before and it broke things"**
→ "That's a common experience. The difference is in the error handling and monitoring. I don't just build — I monitor and fix before you notice."
