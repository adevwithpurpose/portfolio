## What Is Service-as-a-Software?

This is not a retainer for your hours. This is a subscription to outcomes.

**Service-as-a-Software** (coined by AI practitioner Kevin Badi and popularized by Forbes, Thoughtworks, and Deloitte in 2025-2026) is the shift from selling *tools* to selling *complete outcomes*. Your AI agents don't just process data — they run the operation. You are not hiring a developer; you are subscribing to a function.

For Shopify stores, this means: "I pay $X/month and my abandoned cart recovery runs, my customer support is handled, my order status updates are automatic — and I never have to think about it."

**The mental model shift:**
| Old: Freelancer | New: AI Service Provider |
|-----------------|--------------------------|
| "I'll build this for you" | "This runs for you, always" |
| Hours as product | Outcomes as product |
| Project revenue | Recurring revenue |
| Reactive | Proactive |
| You manage the tool | The tool serves you |
