This folder is the working knowledge base for Safeer's portfolio, positioning, client acquisition, offers, and public proof assets.

## Source-of-truth structure

### 00-admin/
Identity, preferences, and high-level operating notes.

### 01-strategy/
Positioning, channel strategy, and high-level plans. Files prefixed with `legacy-` are source material, not the current public direction.

### 02-research/
Market/channel research and claims that may need verification. Files prefixed with `legacy-` are niche-specific but preserved.

### 03-offers/
Offer architecture, onboarding, pricing notes, discovery process, and client delivery playbooks.

### 04-outreach/
Templates and channel-specific outreach copy. Legacy/niche templates are preserved but should be generalized before public use.

### 05-linkedin-presence/
LinkedIn authority project, profile copy, proof assets, and related research.

### 06-progress/
Track weekly activity, active leads, decisions, and revenue/proof progress.
